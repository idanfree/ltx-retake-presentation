Product Motion Assignment

Introducing Retake through motion.

Three storyboard directions exploring how to educate users about a new feature that lets them change only one part of a generated video, without regenerating the entire result.

Option 01 — Guided Walkthrough

A step-by-step educational flow that introduces Retake through a focused modal walkthrough. This approach is more explanatory and works well for first-time discovery.

Option 02 — Contextual Tooltip Flow

A lighter contextual onboarding experience that teaches Retake inside the workspace with minimal interruption to the creative flow.

Option 03 — Visual Demo Flow

A polished product demo approach that shows the feature through an example video, then demonstrates the precise retake workflow and final outcome.

Final Thoughts

The motion should make the value instantly clear.

Across all three directions, the main idea is the same: Retake gives users precision and control. Instead of starting over, they can isolate a moment, describe the change, and keep the rest of the video intact.
